# Employee Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the standards of behavior expected of all employees regardless of position, role, or tenure.

## Equal Opportunity

The company is committed to equal opportunity employment without regard to race, gender, age, religion, disability, or national origin. We maintain a diverse and inclusive workplace.

## Data Protection

### Personal Information

Employee personal information, including social security numbers and driver's license information, must be handled with strict confidentiality.

All PII data must be:
- Encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- Stored in secure systems with role-based access control
- Accessed only on a need-to-know basis

### Data Sharing

We may share data with third party vendors for benefits administration. Written employee consent is required before sharing personal information with external partners.

### Data Retention

Employee records are retained for 7 years after separation per legal requirements. After this period, data must be securely destroyed.

## IT Security

### Password Policy

All employees must follow the password policy:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- No password reuse for 12 cycles

### System Access

Access to company systems requires:
- Multi-factor authentication
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular access reviews

All network traffic must be encrypted using TLS 1.3 or higher.

## Workplace Conduct

### Harassment Policy

The company maintains zero tolerance for harassment of any kind. Any hostile work environment or bullying behavior will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

### Reporting

Employees should report concerns through:
- Direct supervisor
- Human Resources
- Anonymous ethics hotline

## Termination Procedure

The termination process includes:
- HR review and documentation
- Exit interview
- Return of company property
- Access revocation within 24 hours

## Compliance

### GDPR Compliance

For employees in the EU, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements including:
- Right to access
- Right to erasure
- Data portability

### Accessibility

The company is committed to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards for all internal systems. Accommodations are available upon request.

## Acknowledgment

All employees must acknowledge receipt and understanding of this Code of Conduct annually.
